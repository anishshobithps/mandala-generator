# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Casual creative explorers—people who enjoy generative art, play with symmetry and patterns, and want to create beautiful mandala designs. No prior experience needed; users range from curious newcomers to artists exploring algorithmic creativity.

## Product Purpose

A creative playground for generating and exploring mandala patterns. Users adjust rings, symmetry, complexity, colors, and scale to create unique geometric artworks. The goal is to make generative art accessible, fun, and delightful—a tool that feels like an instrument for creative play, not a complex configuration interface.

## Brand Personality

Warm, creative, playful. The tool invites experimentation and wonder. It feels approachable, not intimidating; exploratory, not prescriptive. Fun and generative creativity come first—the mandala is the star, the UI supports it.

## Anti-references

- SaaS corporate dashboards (clinical, hierarchical, cold)
- Cluttered or overwhelming control interfaces (too many visible options at once)
- Minimalist coldness (sterile, corporate restraint)
- Spiritual or mystical heavy-handedness (avoid clichéd spiritual aesthetics)

## Design Principles

1. **Play over precision.** The tool is for creative experimentation, not exact technical configuration. Encourage discovery and randomization alongside direct control.
2. **Context-aware simplicity.** Show controls that matter; hide or group complexity. Adapt layout to device and context.
3. **The mandala is the product.** The UI supports the art, not the other way around. Maximize canvas space on desktop; mobile-first approach on smaller screens.
4. **Inclusive by default.** Color-blind friendly palettes, support reduced motion, keyboard navigation, and mobile accessibility from the start.
5. **Warmth and humanity.** Avoid corporate sterility. Typography, spacing, and interaction should feel inviting and alive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG AA** as a minimum target.
- **Color-blind friendly:** Avoid red/green combos; provide luminance contrast. Include icons + text labels for state changes.
- **Reduced motion:** All animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; critical interactions don't depend on motion.
- **Keyboard navigation:** Full keyboard support for all controls; focus visible and logical tab order.
- **Mobile-first:** Responsive design prioritizes touch-friendly controls and readability on small screens.
